Refactor the user dashboard and posts page to remove heavy visual elements (especially the dark blue banner) and replace them with a soft, clean, white-dominant design. The goal is a calm, modern interface that feels lightweight, readable, and distraction-free for field users.

---

1. REMOVE / REPLACE DARK BLUE HEADER

* Remove the full-width dark blue "Welcome back" banner.

Replace with a soft, minimal header section:

Option A (preferred):

* No background (transparent)
* Just text and avatar, left-aligned

Option B (if a card is preferred):

* background: #FFFFFF
* border: 1px solid #E5E7EB
* border-radius: 16px
* padding: 20–24px

8. COLOR SYSTEM (KEEP SIMPLE)

Use ONLY:

* Background: #F9FAFB

* Borders: #E5E7EB

  Blue: #3B82F6 OR Green: #10B981 (choose one primary)

Avoid using too many accent colors.

---

9. MICRO INTERACTIONS (MAKE IT FEEL SLICK)

* Add global:
  transition: all 0.2s ease

* Card hover:
  slight lift (translateY -1px)

* Buttons:
  smooth hover + subtle shadow

* Icons:
  soft hover color change

---

10. FINAL RESULT

The dashboard and posts page should feel:

* Light and breathable
* Minimal and modern
* Easy to scan quickly in the field
* Calm (not heavy or saturated)
* Consistent across all users

Focus on removing visual weight, increasing spacing, and using softer colors instead of bold blocks.
