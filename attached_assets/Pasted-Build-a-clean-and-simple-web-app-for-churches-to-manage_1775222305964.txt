Build a clean and simple web app for churches to manage missionary reporting.

The app should have two types of users:

    Missionaries
    Church Admins 

Missionaries should be able to log in and submit reports that include:

    Title
    Description (story-style update)
    Category (such as church planting, leadership training, humanitarian work, or education)
    Date
    Optional photos 

Church Admins should be able to log in and:

    View all submitted reports
    Filter reports by missionary and category
    Track recent activity 

Add a timeline feature where all reports appear in chronological order as a feed.

Each report in the timeline should display as a clean, visually appealing card that includes:

    Title
    Short summary
    Missionary name
    Date
    Category
    Photo preview (if available) 

The timeline should feel like a simple social media feed that is easy to scroll and quickly shows updates and impact.

Make the timeline the main homepage/dashboard so users immediately see recent activity.

Reports should feel like meaningful story updates, not just forms.

Add the ability to click on a report from the timeline to view a full detailed page with the complete story, all photos, and additional information.

Include a simple missionary profile page that shows their basic info and a history of their submitted reports.

Allow missionaries to upload multiple photos per report and display them nicely in the timeline and detail view.

Add a simple “recent activity” or “latest updates” section highlighting the most recent reports.

Make the app feel personal and story-driven, highlighting impact, testimonies, and real-world updates rather than just data.

Add a simple way to highlight key impact metrics in reports, such as number of people reached, leaders trained, or communities served.

Ensure the design works well on both desktop and mobile devices, especially for users in low-bandwidth environments.

The overall design should be modern, clean, and very easy to use for non-technical users.
