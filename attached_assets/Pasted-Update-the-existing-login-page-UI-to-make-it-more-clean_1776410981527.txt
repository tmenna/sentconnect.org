Update the existing login page UI to make it more clean, white-dominant, and lightweight while maintaining a modern look.

Goals:

* Prioritize a white and light UI for better readability and faster perceived load time
* Reduce heavy visual elements, especially dark colors
* Keep the interface simple and distraction-free for field users

Design Updates:

1. Left Panel (Brand Section):

* Lighten the current dark blue background to a softer blue (e.g., #3b82f6 or similar)
* Optionally apply a very subtle gradient (light blue tones only)
* Reduce the width of the left panel to about 35–40% of the screen
* Keep the content (logo, headline, bullet points), but slightly reduce text contrast if too sharp
* Ensure text remains readable but less visually heavy

2. Right Panel (Login Section):

* Keep a clean white background (#ffffff)
* Optionally use a very light gray background (#f9fafb) for subtle contrast
* Maintain centered login card with soft shadow (reduce heavy shadows if present)
* Keep spacing generous and consistent

3. Top Bar / Header:

* Remove or avoid strong blue top bars
* Use white or very light gray background
* Keep branding minimal (logo only or small accent color)

4. CTA Button:

* Keep the green “Sign In” button as the primary accent color
* Ensure good contrast and hover state
* Avoid adding additional competing colors

5. Visual Consistency:

* Add a subtle divider or smooth transition between left and right panels
* Use consistent border radius (rounded corners)
* Keep typography simple and clean (no heavy font weights)

6. Performance Considerations:

* Avoid heavy images or large gradients
* Use solid colors or very subtle gradients only
* Ensure fast load and responsiveness on low-bandwidth environments

7. Multi-Tenant Readiness:

* Structure the left panel so it can be easily customized per organization (logo, text, color)
* Keep the right login panel consistent across all tenants

Deliverable:

* Updated responsive login page UI
* Clean, minimal, and modern design with improved balance between branding and usability
