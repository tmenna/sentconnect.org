Refine the organization signup page into a cleaner, more premium minimalist SaaS onboarding experience. Keep the current structure (centered single-column signup flow, pricing card, form, Stripe CTA), but redesign it to feel smoother, more modern, and visually consistent with the rest of SentConnect.

Design goals:

* More white space
* Larger typography and stronger visual hierarchy
* Softer, cleaner form styling
* Modern but simple
* Feels trustworthy and premium
* Consistent with the product dashboard aesthetic

### Layout

* Keep a centered single-column layout (max-width ~560-620px)
* Increase vertical spacing between sections
* Add more breathing room around the form
* Use a very light off-white background (#FAFBFD or similar)
* Keep page mostly white/minimal, not heavy blue

### Top Area

Refine header:

* Larger headline:
  “Create your organization”
* Bigger, cleaner typography (bold but elegant)
* Improve subtitle styling with lighter secondary text

Top logo should feel lighter/minimal, aligned with page center.

### Pricing Card

Redesign pricing card as premium feature card:

* Soft blue gradient (subtle, not loud)
* Large $30 price treatment
* More padding
* 20–24px rounded corners
* Soft shadow
* Improve typography hierarchy:

  * Small label:
    SIMPLE, TRANSPARENT PRICING
  * Large:
    $30 / month
  * Supporting line:
    Unlimited users • Media sharing • Secure access

Optional:
Add subtle icon row or checkmarks.

### Form Styling

Make the form feel less “input boxes stacked” and more refined:

* Larger input heights (48–52px)
* Softer borders
* 12–14px radius
* Better label spacing
* Improve section headings:
  Organization Details
  Your Account

Optional:
Place form inside very subtle white card with shadow:

* bg-white
* rounded-2xl
* soft shadow-sm

### Button

Upgrade CTA button:

* Full-width
* Taller (50–54px)
* Slight gradient or richer blue
* Rounded-xl
* Stronger text:
  Start for $30/month →
  or
  Create Organization →

Hover:

* subtle lift
* softer shadow

### Trust Row (important)

Improve the trust icons section under button:
Three compact trust points in one row:

🔒 Secure payments (Powered by Stripe)
↺ Cancel anytime
🌍 Built for mission teams

Use cleaner icons, muted text, better spacing.

### Visual Style

Use:

* Larger fonts overall
* More generous spacing
* Softer shadows
* Fewer borders
* Cleaner typography
* Reduce visual noise

Reference aesthetic:

* Stripe onboarding
* Notion/Linear style simplicity
* Modern SaaS signup page

### Important

Do NOT over-design.
Keep it minimal, calm, white, elegant, and conversion-focused.

Enhance polish, consistency, spacing, typography, and trust.
Make it feel like a production SaaS onboarding experience.
